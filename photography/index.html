swipe to scroll

Expressing the world through landscape and wildlife photography. Sharing out of the ordinary moments and hidden wonders to encourage conscientious exploration, the preservation of nature, and the proliferation of photography as a medium of art.

I was inspired to become a photographer in 2019 as I embarked on a road trip from San Francisco to British Columbia and captured nature along the coast with a used camera that was gifted to me. Since then, I have embarked on many mindful expeditions with friends and family including a week-long backpacking trip in Yosemite and summiting Exit Glacier in Alaska.
